History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small meet-up of tech enthusiasts and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of participants.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire and equip the next generation of tech leaders through insightful talks, networking, and hands-on sessions.

  • Elon Script – Developer of the first quantum browser engine.
